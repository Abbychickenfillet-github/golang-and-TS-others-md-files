重點整理

履歷／商務訊息常見修正

  • ❌ I send my link curriculum vitae to you first
    ✅ I'm sending you the link to my CV/resume first. 或 Here is the link to my CV to start with.
  • 英文說 "link to my CV"（不是 "link CV"），習慣把 you 放前面，或用 "Here is..." 開頭更像日常對話。
  • ❌ After I editing it, I will notify you to see if it's OK
    ✅ Once I finish editing it, I'll let you know so you can take a look. 或 After I edit it, I'll notify you to check if it's okay.
  • After I 後面接原形動詞（edit），不能直接加 -ing（editing）。
  • 用 let you know / take a look 比單用 notify 更親切、不生硬。
