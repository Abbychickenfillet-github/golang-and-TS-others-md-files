# 英文寫作與文法

跨對話累積的英文寫作／文法修正筆記。

## 重點整理

### 履歷／商務訊息常見修正

- ❌ `I send my link curriculum vitae to you first`
  ✅ `I'm sending you the link to my CV/resume first.` 或 `Here is the link to my CV to start with.`
  - 英文說 <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">"link to my CV"</mark>（不是 "link CV"），習慣把 you 放前面，或用 "Here is..." 開頭更像日常對話。
- ❌ `After I editing it, I will notify you to see if it's OK`
  ✅ `Once I finish editing it, I'll let you know so you can take a look.` 或 `After I edit it, I'll notify you to check if it's okay.`
  - <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">`After I` 後面接原形動詞（edit），不能直接加 -ing（editing）</mark>。
  - 用 <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">`let you know` / `take a look`</mark> 比單用 `notify` 更親切、不生硬。

### 字詞搭配（collocation）與片語修正
原句：`STD is a mutual harm. No doubts. If in a sex intercourse men force women to take a lot there should be a compensation`

- <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">"mutual harm"</mark> 聽起來像雙方故意互相傷害。要表達「對雙方都有害」用 <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">"affects both partners" / "harms both parties"</mark>。
- <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">"No doubts"</mark> → 固定片語是單數 <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">"No doubt" / "Without a doubt"</mark>。
- <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">"in a sex intercourse"</mark> 不道地 → <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">"during intercourse" / "during sexual activity"</mark>。
- <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">"take a lot"</mark> 語意模糊 → 視意思用 <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">"impose risks on..."</mark>（把風險強加於）或 <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">"force ... to bear the consequences"</mark>（強迫承受後果）。

口語版改寫：`There's no doubt that STDs harm both partners. If a man forces a woman into unsafe sex or passes it to her, she deserves compensation.`
